Mistriotis lab receives BCRFA research grant

Panagiotis Mistriotis December 03, 2021

The Mistriotis lab is very excited to receive funding from the Breast Cancer Research Foundation of Alabama (BCRFA). Our lab will integrate bioengineering tools, genome-wide genetic screens and in vivo models to discover new determinants of triple negative breast cancer cell invasion.
